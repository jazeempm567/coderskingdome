What's this all about?

What if your child could code their own project — even if they’ve never coded before?

Stay tuned for the most exciting International Scratch Coding Competition! 💫

  • For kids aged 6–15 years
  • Beginner-friendly with free webinars
  • Showcase your coding skills to a global audience
  • Win exciting prizes and e-certificates

🗓️ Key Dates

  • Registration Opens: 10th September 2025
  • Registration Closes: 15th October 2025
  • Theme Announcement: 9th October 2025
  • Submission Deadline: 9th November 2025 (11:59 PM IST)
  • Finalist Announcement: 15th November 2025
  • Grand Finale: December 2025

Registration Fee: ₹399

🎯 Competition Categories

  • Junior (6–9 years): Scratch Animation / Game
  • Senior (10–15 years): Scratch Game

🧑‍⚖️ Submission & Finale

Participants must submit their original Scratch project before 9th November 2025.

Selected finalists will create and present a new project to the jury at the Grand Finale.

📌 Rules

  • Each participant may submit only one project.
  • Projects must be the original work of the child.
  • Only projects created after the theme announcement will be accepted.
  • Scratch projects must be shared on scratch.mit.edu and made public.
  • Categories must be strictly followed.
  • Teamwork is not allowed; this is an individual competition.

📄 Terms & Conditions

  • Open to children aged 6–15 years globally.
  • This is a private competition organized by CodersKingdome.
  • Registration fee is non-refundable.
  • All participants get e-certificates. Winners receive prizes.
  • The jury’s decision will be final and binding.
  • Prizes are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash.
  • Physical prizes sent via courier are not the responsibility of the organizers.
  • Organizers may change rules/timelines and will inform participants via email/WhatsApp.
